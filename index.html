Bio

    I started coding in April, 2021, so this is all still pretty new to me. I have a bachelor's degree in Anthropology from the University of Minnesota, Morris, with a minor in Creative Writing. I never once expected to go into coding. I thought I would be doing something more...hands-on.
    I grew up around a carpenter dad, and I've built several of my tables and quite a few cutting boards. I love those things, but I love those things as hobbies. I don't care to do it as a career. I know quite a few programmers, one of whom is a very close friend of mine. I started the MIT Professional Certificate in Coding after some serious discussions with him. And now, hopefully, the rest is history.
    Javascript makes sense to me--mostly--but I really enjoy design elements, putting the CSS together with the HTML. So far, that's my favorite, and I can't wait to learn more!
