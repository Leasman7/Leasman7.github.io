Bio

    I started coding in April, 2021, and it's hard to believe that it's already been over a year. I have a bachelor's degree in Anthropology from the University of Minnesota, Morris, with a minor in Creative Writing. Back then, I never expected to go into coding. I thought I hated computers. Turns out that couldn't be farther from the truth.
    I grew up around a carpenter dad, and I've built several of my tables and quite a few cutting boards. I love those things, but I love those things as hobbies. I don't care to do it as a career. I know quite a few programmers, one of whom is a very close friend of mine. I started the MIT Professional Certificate in Coding after some serious discussions with him. And now, hopefully, the rest is history.
    I look forward to using my skills as a full-stack developer. I have experience in Javascript (especially using React), HTML5, CSS, Express, and Node.js. I also have used multiple different platforms, including GitHub, AWS, Heroku, and Firebase. Building things is my jam, and I can't wait to make an impact on the world of technology!
